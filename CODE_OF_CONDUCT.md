# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

- Be respectful and constructive
- Focus on what's best for the project
- Assume good intent
- Give credit where it's due
- Keep PRs and issues focused and actionable

## Not acceptable

- Personal attacks or harassment of any kind
- Discrimination based on race, gender, sexuality, religion, nationality, disability, or any other characteristic
- Posting others' private information
- Sustained disruption of discussions
- Low-quality PRs that don't follow the project's conventions or that the contributor can't explain when asked
- Spamming issues or PRs

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or ban users who violate this code. Severe or repeated violations will result in permanent bans.

Report issues privately to the maintainer via a direct message on GitHub.

## Scope

This code applies to all project spaces: the repository, issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other forum directly associated with Klonode.
